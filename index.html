Vodafone Group plc is a British multinational telecommunications conglomerate, with headquarters in London and Newbury, Berkshire. It predominantly operates services in the regions of Asia, Africa, Europe, and Oceania. (Wikipedia)

CEO: Nick Read

Chairman: Gerard Kleisterlee

Headquartered In: London

Founders: Ernest Harrison and Gerry Whent
